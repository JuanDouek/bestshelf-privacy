Privacy Policy for BestShelf

Effective Date: December 2024
Last Updated: December 2024

Overview

BestShelf is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our movie and TV show collection app.

Information We DON'T Collect

BestShelf does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information, including but not limited to:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number)
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Location information
  • Device identifiers
  • Browsing history or behavior

Local Data Storage

BestShelf stores your movie collection and app preferences locally on your device only, within the app's secure sandbox folder. This data includes:

  • Your movie and TV show collection
  • Custom ratings and reviews you create
  • App preferences and settings
  • Custom lists and categories

This information:

  • Remains entirely on your device
  • Is never transmitted to our servers or third parties
  • Is protected by macOS sandbox security
  • Can be deleted by removing the app

Third-Party Services

BestShelf connects to third-party services to provide movie information:

  • The Movie Database (TMDB) - for movie metadata and posters
  • JustWatch - for streaming availability information

These services have their own privacy policies. BestShelf only requests publicly available movie information and does not send any personal data to these services.

Data Security

Your data is protected by:

  • macOS app sandboxing technology
  • Local-only storage (no cloud transmission)
  • No third-party tracking or analytics

Children's Privacy

BestShelf is safe for users of all ages as we do not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted in this document with an updated "Last Updated" date.
